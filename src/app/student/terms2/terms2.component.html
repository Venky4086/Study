Test instructions
  1. Total duration of this test is 180 minutes.
  2. There will be only 75 questions in this test.
  3. Question No. 1 to 20 in each section are Single Choice Type questions which are allotted 10 (ten) marks for each question answered correctly and 1 (One) mark.
  4. In all the sections, No deduction from the total score will be made if no response is indicated.
  5. Please use ONE device, browser and tab while taking this test. As an anti-cheating measure, switching between tabs, browsers or devices will result in loss of your test data.
  6. To get access to your Advanced Test Feedback, ensure that you have:
    • spent at least 50% of the total test time .
    • attempted 60% of the total number of questions in the test.
  7. These are coloured Indicators.
    •  Answered Questions.
    •  Not Answered.
    •  Mark For Review.
    •  Not Visited.
  8. If the power goes out in the middle of the test, the test will start again from the beginning.
